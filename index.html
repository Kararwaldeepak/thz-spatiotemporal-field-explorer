1. Time images

The detector records one two-dimensional electric-field image at every delay position. Stacking the images produces E(x, y, t).

2. Fourier transform

An FFT is applied along the time axis independently at every spatial pixel. Each FFT bin becomes one frequency-resolved image.

3. Important distinction

The delay-stage time step is the sampling interval, not the full instrument temporal resolution. Detector response and gate-pulse duration can further limit the experiment.
